The constituency is the atomic unit of the Great Withholding network.
Every constituency page with a live human on it is a node.
The network is the sum of its live nodes.

**Three steps:**

1. Find your constituency at [WikiTacticalVoting](https://wikitacticalvoting.miraheze.org/wiki/Main_Page)
2. Claim your constituency page — even if you are the first person there
3. Do ONE real-world task this week (Proof of Life)

**What counts as Proof of Life:**

- Put a date/time/location for a first meet-up (pub / café / kitchen table)
- Print a leaflet or share a simple message locally
- Start a ground-game list: canvass route, street stall, local WhatsApp group
- Post here what you did
