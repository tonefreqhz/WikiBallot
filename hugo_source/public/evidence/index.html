Tier 3 — Illustrative construct

On the basis of the above sourced and estimated components, and drawing on the analytical framework developed by Brett Christophers (Rentier Capitalism, Verso, 2020) and Michael Hudson (J is for Junk Economics, ISLET, 2017), an illustrative estimate of total annual rentier extraction falls in the range of $10–18 trillion per year. This figure is not sourced from a single primary dataset — no such dataset exists. It is constructed from the Tier 1 and Tier 2 components above and should be read as an order-of-magnitude indicator, not a filed statistic.

At $10T low estimate against $4–5T in global social expenditure the ratio is approximately 2:1. At $18T high estimate the ratio is approximately 3.6:1. The direction of the argument does not depend on the precision of the aggregate.
